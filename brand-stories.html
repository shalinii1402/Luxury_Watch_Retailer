A Century of Excellence

The history of CHRONÉA is written in the language of time.

Our Journey

1924

Founded in Le Locle, Switzerland, with a vision to create the world's most accurate chronometer.

1956

Introduced the first automatic movement with a 72-hour power reserve, revolutionizing the industry.

1980

Awarded the prestigious "Grand Prix d'Horlogerie" for design innovation.

2024

Celebrating 100 years of defining luxury, with the launch of the Centennial Moonphase series.

Uncompromising Craftsmanship

Every CHRONÉA timepiece is a testament to patience and precision. Our master watchmakers spend hundreds of hours hand-polishing a single movement. We believe that true luxury is not just seen, but felt—in the weight of the case, the smooth sweep of the seconds hand, and the flawless engagement of the crown.

Innovation Meets Tradition

While we honor the techniques of the past, we are driven by the possibilities of the future. Our laboratories are constantly exploring new materials—from ultra-light titanium alloys to scratch-resistant unscratchable ceramics. This fusion of old-world skill and new-world technology allows us to create watches that are not only beautiful but technically superior.

Our Enduring Values

Integrity, exclusivity, and legacy. These are the pillars upon which CHRONÉA stands. We limit our annual production to ensure that every watch meets our rigorous standards. Owning a CHRONÉA is not merely a purchase; it is an entry into a lineage of individuals who appreciate the profound value of time itself.
